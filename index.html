Planet Facts

  • Earth: The only known planet with liquid water on its surface and life as we know it.
  • Jupiter: The largest planet, famous for the Great Red Spot and dozens of moons.
  • Saturn: Known for its stunning ring system made mostly of ice and rock particles.
  • Neptune: A cold, windy ice giant with the fastest winds in the Solar System.
