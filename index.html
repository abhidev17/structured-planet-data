Pluto overview

Pluto is included as a dwarf planet to show how smaller bodies are classified in the outer Solar System.
Planet Name Classification Diameter (km) Distance from Sun (million km) Average Temperature (°C) Number of Moons
♇ Pluto Dwarf Planet 2,376 5,906.4 -225 5

Pluto is classified as a dwarf planet because it orbits the Sun, is round due to its own gravity, but has not cleared other objects from its orbital region.

Planet facts

Quick facts by world

☿ Mercury

  • Closest planet to the Sun
  • Shortest year in the Solar System

♀ Venus

  • Hottest planet
  • Rotates backwards compared to most planets

🌍 Earth

  • Only known planet with life
  • 71% covered by water

♂ Mars

  • Known as the Red Planet
  • Home to Olympus Mons, the tallest volcano

♃ Jupiter

  • Largest planet
  • Has the Great Red Spot storm

♄ Saturn

  • Famous for its rings
  • Could float in water due to low density

⛢ Uranus

  • Rotates on its side
  • Has a blue-green color due to methane

♆ Neptune

  • Windiest planet
  • Farthest official planet from the Sun

♇ Pluto

  • Former ninth planet
  • Reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006
